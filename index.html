Common Support Topics

Widgets are not updating

Try removing the widget and adding it again, then reopen the app once to refresh local widget data. If the issue continues, email support with your iPhone model and iOS version.

Subscription features are locked

Make sure your App Store purchase is active on the same Apple ID used for the purchase. If needed, reopen the app and allow time for StoreKit to refresh access.

Reminders are not appearing

Check that notification or reminder permissions are enabled in iOS Settings and confirm the reminder options inside the app are still turned on.

Saved verses or journal content seem missing

Bible Widgets stores certain content locally on your device. If content appears missing after reinstalling the app or changing devices, some locally stored information may not be recoverable.
