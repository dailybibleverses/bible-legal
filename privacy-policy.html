Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Bible Verses: Daily Prayer respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

Information We Handle

Bible Verses: Daily Prayer may store certain information locally on your device in order to provide app features. This information is kept on your device and is not stored on our servers, including:

  • liked or saved verses
  • browsing history
  • verse highlights
  • daily reflection or journal text you choose to enter
  • app settings such as themes, widget preferences, refresh frequency, and reminder settings

At this time, journal entries, saved verses, highlights, browsing history, and settings are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

We do not require account registration to use the app.

Subscriptions

Some features may require a paid subscription. Subscription purchases, renewals, and billing are handled by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit.

We do not collect or store your full payment card information.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions when you choose to use certain features:

  • Photo Library access for saving quote or verse images
  • Reminder or alarm-related access for devotional reminders

Widgets

Bible Verses: Daily Prayer may use Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets. Limited data and settings may be shared between the main app and widget extension using Apple’s App Group mechanisms.

How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • provide app features
  • save your preferences and content locally
  • enable widgets and reminders
  • verify subscription access
  • respond to support requests

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with third-party advertisers.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking for advertising purposes.

We may rely on Apple services and system frameworks to operate the app.
